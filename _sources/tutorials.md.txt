# Tutorials and Courses

Here is a list of additional MICOM learning resources.

### Harnessing Our Inner Ecology To Track and Treat Disease 2020

In 2020 we held an online course at the [Institute for Systems Biology](https://isbscience.org)
which included an introduction to MICOM. All materials are still available and you
will not need to install anything to follow the course. For the full course materials
refer to the [course homepage](https://isbscience.org/microbiome2020/).

- video: [Modeling microbiota-wide metabolism with MICOM - Pt. 1](https://youtu.be/63PnJwixXGc)
- video: [Modeling microbiota-wide metabolism with MICOM - Pt. 1](https://youtu.be/GfaglaE1br0)
- [course slides](https://gibbons-lab.github.io/isb_course_2020/micom/)
- [course Google colab notebook](https://colab.research.google.com/github/Gibbons-Lab/isb_course_2020/blob/master/micom.ipynb)

### q2-micom community tutorial

In case you want to use MICOM with Qiime2 plugin (`q2-micom`), the q2-micom tutorial
is the best way to get started!

- [`q2-micom` community tutorial](https://micom-dev.github.io/q2-micom/)
