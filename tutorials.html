Here is a list of additional MICOM learning resources.

Harnessing Our Inner Ecology To Track and Treat Disease 2020¶

In 2020 we held an online course at the Institute for Systems Biology which included an introduction to MICOM. All materials are still available and you will not need to install anything to follow the course. For the full course materials refer to the course homepage.
